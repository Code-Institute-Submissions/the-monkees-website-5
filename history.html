Our History; Where we've been to where we are

Founded in 1966 specifically for the American TV series "The Monkees", which aired from 1966 - 1968, The Monkees have become a household name with concerts worldwide and albums released consistently for the past 50+ years.

While the band initially started with four members (Michael Nesmith, Micky Dolenz, Peter Tork and Davy Jones), they have suffered tragic loss in recent years with the deaths of Davy Jones in 2012 and Peter Tork in 2019. The surviving members remain active and are looking to increase their brand awareness and expand their services offering!

The new album "GOOD TIMES!" was released in 2016 and was an instant success, rocketing to the bestselling CD on Amazon on its release!

Over 75 million records worldwide to date!
